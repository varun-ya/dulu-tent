At Dulu Tent & Decorator, we believe every celebration deserves to be extraordinary. Based in Aminpatty, Nagaon, Assam, we specialize in transforming weddings, birthdays, receptions, corporate gatherings, and special occasions into unforgettable experiences.

From elegant decorations and premium tent arrangements to complete event planning and flawless execution, our experienced team ensures every detail is handled with creativity, precision, and care.

Whether you're planning an intimate family function or a grand celebration, we are committed to bringing your vision to life while making the entire journey stress-free.

Luxury Wedding Planners

Weddings

Our weddings are defined by creativity, precision and quiet elegance. For the discerning and the well-travelled, our luxury wedding planners and producers craft celebrations that honour personal stories while reflecting the highest standards of service and offering an unparalleled experience.

We anticipate and refine every element so the experience feels effortless, allowing our clients to be entirely present while we manage the complexity behind the scenes. What unfolds is a wedding shaped by personal nuance, elevated by beauty, exceeding expectations.

Luxury Parties

Parties

Our parties range from intimate gatherings to large-scale celebrations, each crafted with imagination, innovation and a sense of the unexpected. From milestone birthdays and anniversaries to bat and bar mitzvahs, festive occasions and seasonal events, we design experiences that feel engaging, distinctive and deeply personal.

Whether for legacy or new clients, we immerse ourselves in the character of every event. These are parties elevated by thoughtful storytelling, refined aesthetics and the assurance of seamless orchestration.

Luxury Destinations

Destinations

From our London base, we plan destination celebrations across the UK, Europe and beyond. Two decades of experience has taken us to private islands and coastlines; vineyards and lakeside properties; castles and châteaux; royal palaces and stately homes; private villas and country estates; the world’s most exclusive hotels and resorts; superyachts; UNESCO World Heritage Sites; and prestigious art galleries and museums.

We guide our clients toward the locations that best reflect their vision and the character of the celebration they wish to create.

Dedicated and Resolute
